Living Data: Hear Your Life Differently

Join us for Living Data: A Sonification Concert

This free, live, participatory event is equal parts science, story, and sound.

Real data becomes live music, poems turn into textures, and you’ll listen to the world in a totally new way.

With performances by Walker Smith, Auralee Walmer, Tristan Peng, and Jordan Wirfs-Brock, as well as opportunities for audience participation, this is not your usual concert.

Details

  • Friday, November 7
  • 6–8 PM
  • Chism Recital Hall (Hall of Music, Whitman College)
  • FREE and open to all
  • With reception featuring light snacks to follow
