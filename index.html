Join us for the Living Data Concert

This free, live, participatory event is equal parts science, story, and sound. Real data becomes live music, poems turn into textures, and you’ll listen to the world in a totally new way.

With performances by Walker Smith, Auralee Walmer, Tristan Peng, and Jordan Wirfs-Brock, as well as opportunities for audience participation, this is not your usual concert.
